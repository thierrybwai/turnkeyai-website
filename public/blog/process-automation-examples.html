12 Process Automation Examples for Australian Businesses (with ROI)

📅 March 31, 2026 👤 TurnkeyAI Team ⏱️ 11 min read

Most business owners think "automation" means big, expensive, take-months-to-implement systems.

So they never do it.

The truth? You're probably wasting $100+ per day on work that could be automated in a week.

This guide shows 12 real, simple automation examples. For each one, I'll show you what the process is, how much time it wastes, exactly how to automate it, and how much you'll save.

1. Email Management & Triage

The Process

Admin or manager reads 100+ emails daily, categorizes them, responds to routine ones, flags urgent ones for boss.

Current Pain

  • Takes 3-4 hours/day just reading/sorting
  • Important emails get mixed with spam
  • Slow responses (boss sees them hours later)
  • Same questions answered repeatedly

How to Automate

  • AI reads all incoming emails
  • Categorizes by priority and type
  • Auto-responds to common questions
  • Flags urgent emails for human (with summary)
Monthly Savings: $4,200 (15 hours/week × 4 weeks × $70/hour)
Setup: 3-5 days | ROI: 6-12 weeks

2. Lead Capture & CRM Entry

The Process

Leads come in via forms, emails, phone calls. Someone manually enters them into CRM.

How to Automate

  • Forms auto-populate CRM
  • Email leads auto-captured and parsed
  • Duplicate detection (prevents 10+ duplicates/week)
  • Lead scoring (hot leads flagged immediately)
Monthly Savings: $2,800 (10 hours/week × 4 weeks × $70/hour)
Plus: Faster lead follow-up (saves 20+ hours response time)
Setup: 1-2 days | ROI: 3-6 weeks

3. Invoice & Payment Processing

How to Automate

  • Invoice received → auto-extracted
  • Matched to PO automatically
  • Flagged if discrepancies exist
  • Approved invoices → auto-entered into accounting
  • Payment created (for you to approve)
Monthly Savings: $3,600 (12 hours/week × 4 weeks × $75/hour)
Plus: Error prevention $500-$1,000/month
Plus: Better cash flow
Setup: 4-6 days | ROI: 4-8 weeks
