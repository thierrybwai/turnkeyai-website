12 Process Automation Examples for Australian Businesses (with ROI)

📅 March 31, 2026 👤 TurnkeyAI ⏱️ 11 min read

Most business owners think "automation" means big, expensive systems

So they never do it.

The truth? You're probably wasting $100+ per day on work that could be automated in a week.

1. Email Management & Triage (15+ hours/week)

How to Automate

  • AI reads all incoming emails
  • Categorizes by priority and type
  • Auto-responds to common questions
  • Flags urgent emails for human
Monthly Savings: $4,200 (15 hours/week × 4 weeks × $70/hour)

2. Lead Capture & CRM Entry (10+ hours/week)

How to Automate

  • Forms auto-populate CRM
  • Email leads auto-captured and parsed
  • Duplicate detection
  • Lead scoring (hot leads flagged immediately)
Monthly Savings: $2,800 (10 hours/week × 4 weeks × $70/hour)

3. Invoice & Payment Processing (12+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Invoice received → auto-extracted → matched to PO → approved invoices auto-entered → payment created

Monthly Savings: $3,600 + error prevention $500-$1,000/month

4. Appointment & Reminder Automation (8+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Booking → automatic confirmation → 24-hour reminder → 2-hour reminder → no-show reschedule → follow-up

Monthly Savings: $1,920 + 20-30% reduction in no-shows = $2,000-$5,000/month recovered

5. Report Generation & Distribution (6+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Scheduled job pulls data → auto-formats → generates charts → emails to recipients

Monthly Savings: $1,920

6. Customer Feedback & Issue Tracking (5+ hours/week)

How to Automate: All feedback → centralized inbox → auto-categorized → sentiment analysis → auto-escalation for urgent

Monthly Savings: $1,400

7. Expense Categorization (8+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Expense submitted → automatically categorized → auto-approved if under limit → auto-entered into accounting

Monthly Savings: $2,400

8. Social Media Scheduling & Response (10+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Schedule posts → monitor comments → auto-respond to common questions → compile metrics

Monthly Savings: $2,600

9. HR & Payroll Admin (15+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Digital timesheets → leave requests auto-workflow → payroll auto-calculated → compliance reminders → onboarding automation

Monthly Savings: $4,200

10. Customer Onboarding (20+ hours per customer)

How to Automate: Customer signs up → automatic welcome sequence → auto-create account → auto-schedule call → auto-send docs

$1,400 per customer (20 hours × $70/hour) = 10 customers/month = $14,000/month

11. Inventory Management (12+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Real-time inventory → auto-alert when low → auto-reorder → auto-update all channels

Monthly Savings: $3,120 + fewer oversells/stockouts

12. Lead Nurture & Follow-up (25+ hours/week)

How to Automate: Triggered sequences based on customer action (Day 1: welcome, Day 3: case study, Day 7: social proof, Day 14: offer, Day 30: final)

Monthly Savings: $7,000 + higher conversion

Total: How Much Could YOU Save?

If you automate even 50% of these 12 processes:

  • Time saved: 150+ hours/month
  • Cost saved: $10,500+/month (conservative)
  • Annual impact: $126,000+
