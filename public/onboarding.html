Personality & Goals

Help us shape your agent's character and define what success looks like for your business.

🧬 soul.md — Agent personality
What is soul.md?

Think of soul.md as your AI agent's personality. It defines who your agent is — its name, how it communicates, its tone, and what it should (or should never) do. This is what makes your agent feel like a natural extension of your business rather than a generic bot.

"My agent should be professional but friendly, always sign off with our business name, and never discuss competitors."
Please describe your agent's personality.
🎯 goal.md — Business goals
What is goal.md?

goal.md is where you tell your AI agent what success looks like for your business. Think in numbers and targets — not tasks, but outcomes. Your agent will use these goals to prioritise what it does and how hard it works.

"Get 10 new clients per month", "Book 20 calls per week", "Generate $50,000 in revenue this quarter", "Respond to every lead within 15 minutes."
Please share your business goals.
